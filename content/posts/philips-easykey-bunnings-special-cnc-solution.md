## The Project: High-Value Hardware Meets Custom Engineering
A local homeowner took advantage of the recent **Bunnings special offer** ($248 AUD at the time of publication) for the [Philips Black EasyKey Bluetooth Mortise Handle Lock](https://www.bunnings.com.au/philips-black-easykey-bluetooth-mortise-handle-lock_p0516193). While this hardware offers exceptional value, standard retail kits often assume a standard door frame width, which isn't always the case in many Adelaide homes.

## The Technical Challenge: Narrow Door Frame
Upon arrival, our technical assessment revealed that the customer’s door frame was too narrow to accommodate the standard strike plate included in the Philips retail box. Forced installation of the stock plate would have resulted in an insecure fit or potential damage to the timber frame.

To provide a professional and secure result, the standard solution wouldn't suffice.

## The Solution: Specialized CNC-Machined Strike Plate
At ADE Smart Home, we go beyond standard installation. We utilized **CNC engraving technology** to fabricate a custom strike plate specifically designed for this narrow frame. 

**Technical Improvements:**
1. **Custom Dimensions**: Perfectly matched to the narrow timber profile of the existing frame.
2. **Integrated Ramp Design**: We incorporated a precise beveled "ramp" on the strike plate to ensure the latch retracts smoothly and locks securely without friction.
3. **Flush Finish**: The custom plate allows for a clean, recessed fit that maintains the structural integrity of the door frame.
