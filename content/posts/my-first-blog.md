## The Challenge
The client at **Mawson Lakes** had a standard mechanical deadbolt and wanted a high-tech solution that didn't require carrying keys during their morning jog. They needed something reliable that would fit their modern aesthetic.

## Our Solution
We recommended the **Philips DDL720-MVP**. Because it's a full-auto lock, the installation required precision mortising to ensure the latch and bolt align perfectly without any friction. 

We used specialized tools to ensure the "Flush Finish" that ADE Smart Home is known for.

## The Result
- **Zero-gap finish**: The lock sits perfectly flushed against the timber.
- **Enhanced Security**: Enabled face-unlock and temporary PINs for guests.
- **Client Satisfaction**: "Best investment for our new home," said the owner.

### Technical Specs:
- **Lock Model**: Philips DDL720-MVP
- **Door Type**: Solid Timber
- **Installation Time**: 1.5 Hours
