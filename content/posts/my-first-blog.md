## The Problem: Faulty Developer-Grade Lock
This homeowner in Adelaide was frustrated. The generic digital lock installed during construction by their developer began failing just years later. Intermittent keypad issues and connectivity drops meant they were often worried about being locked out of their own home. 

Developer-grade locks are often chosen for cost rather than longevity or a robust feature set. It was time for a serious, reliable upgrade.

Here is what we started with (The failing lock):
![Failing old lock](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774181144/816e9657abd63114ec909030509a9b55_x4nfqh.jpg)

## Our Solution: The Premium EZVIZ DL05
We recommended and installed the **EZVIZ DL05**. This is a beautifully designed, high-performance smart lock that fits perfectly into modern aesthetics. Crucially, it offers multiple secure access methods that eliminate reliance on a buggy keypad:

1.  **Biometric Fingerprint**: Instant and unique access.
2.  **CPU Card**: Ideal for guests or temporary access.
3.  **Wi-Fi Mobile Management**: Check status and manage users from anywhere via the smartphone app.

## The Process: Zero Gap Precision
Retrofitting requires expertise. We carefully removed the old hardware and prepared the door for the new mortise lock body. We ensure a "Zero Gap" flush finish, meaning the lock sits perfectly against the timber and the automatic mechanism operates without friction.

The homeowner now has total peace of mind. No more carrying keys, no more inputting failing codes. With the EZVIZ app, they can manage temporary access for visitors and review entry logs. It’s a complete upgrade in security, convenience, and style.
