## Technical Overview: Handling Advanced Imported Hardware
At ADE Smart Home, we specialize in the integration of global smart home technologies. A recent project involved the installation of a flagship **Dessmann** smart lock, a high-performance device brought from overseas. 

This model represents the cutting edge of security technology, incorporating 3D Face Recognition, a digital cat’s eye camera, and a high-resolution indoor monitoring screen.

## The Installation Process: Precision and Customization
Installing imported hardware requires a deep understanding of varying international standards. This project demanded significant modification to the timber door to ensure the large-format mortise body was seated securely and operated with perfect mechanical alignment.

**Key Technical Steps:**
