## The Challenge: No Adelaide Installer Would Take the Job
A new homeowner in Adelaide recently brought an exceptionally high-end smart lock with them from China—a flagship **Dessmann** model. This premium device featured cutting-edge tech, including 3D Face Recognition, an integrated digital cat’s eye camera, and an indoor viewing screen.

However, after contacting multiple local locksmiths and handyman services, the customer was repeatedly turned down. Installing imported Chinese smart locks is complex due to different mortise lock standards, the requirement for precise wood mortising, and intricate wiring that local services are not trained to handle.

Finally, they were referred to ADE Smart Home by a friend who trusted our technical expertise.

The existing mechanical setup before the upgrade:
![Dessmann Installation Before](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774183066/13266ede40871216c4418ca37b9083df_x2kp2i.jpg)

## The Process: Precision Mortising and Customization
This was not a standard replacement. We had to prepare the solid timber door for the imported Chinese mortise lock body, which is much larger than standard Australian hardware.

We began by carefully disassembling the existing hardware:
![Disassembling Old Lock](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774183065/a120e5ff28fca743043fd7de1e6b5943_dyn1af.jpg)

Our team then utilized specialized precision mortising tools to ensure a perfect fit for the new lock body:
![Precision Mortising Timber Door](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774183064/7c51f7128ff2601a5278c55987f94a5a_bchaxq.jpg)

Imported locks often require detailed customization. We used a center positioning drill to perfectly align the new hardware:
![Center Positioning Drill](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774183066/a922729957d42af046fd2455201510c6_aoqxo5.jpg)

We then had to cut the connecting rods and spindles to the exact length required by the door’s thickness before inserting the main Dessmann mortise body:
![Cutting Length to Fit](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774183067/5c0b5826446ba51e80073c6557b8b5ec_ry6qad.jpg)

Finally, we carefully connected the complex internal wiring required for the face ID camera and the screen before securing the premium panels:
![Installing Front and Back Panels](https://res.cloudinary.com/dheogdojt/image/upload/v1774183065/61998c6cc46db3ae9e193e7b16eac1fb_ja8y0f.jpg)

## The Result: Seamless High-End Security
The installation was a complete success. We achieved a clean, robust, and fully functional retrofit of the complex imported Dessmann lock. The customer can now enjoy:

1. **3D Face Recognition**: Instant, secure access without touching the lock.
2. **Digital Cat's Eye**: High-definition camera to monitor the entryway.
3. **Indoor Screen**: Real-time video view from *inside* the house.
4. **Mobile App Control**: Seamless integration for remote monitoring.
